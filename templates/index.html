💡 How It Works

When you submit a query, the backend splits the question's matching workload across active cores. It calculates TF-IDF vectors and Cosine Similarity in parallel.

Amdahl's Law Demo: Adjust simulated workload intensity. Smaller intensities will showcase negative speedups due to serialization overhead on Windows, while high intensities will yield massive performance gains!
